# Refunds and Cancellation

## Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel anytime. Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period — for example, if you cancel on the 15th and your billing date is the 1st, you keep Pro features until the next 1st of the month.

After cancellation:
- Your account automatically downgrades to the Free plan
- Files exceeding the 5 GB free limit remain readable but cannot be modified or shared
- You have 90 days to either re-subscribe or download/delete files to get under the free limit
- After 90 days, files exceeding the limit may be deleted to bring your account into compliance

## Refund Policy

- **First-time subscribers:** full refund within 14 days of your first payment, no questions asked.
- **Annual plans:** prorated refund within 30 days of renewal. After 30 days, no refund on annual plans, but you can cancel to prevent the next year's charge.
- **Monthly plans:** no refunds after 14 days, but cancellation takes effect at the end of the period so you don't get charged again.
- **Business plans:** refunds handled by your account manager; contact support to start the process.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Go to Settings → Billing → Payment History
2. Find the charge you want refunded and click "Request Refund"
3. Select a reason from the dropdown (optional, but helps us improve)
4. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days back to your original payment method

If the refund button is not visible, the payment is outside the eligible window. Contact support if you believe this is in error — exceptions are made case-by-case for billing errors, fraudulent charges, or extended service outages.

## Disputes and Chargebacks

Please contact our support team before filing a chargeback with your bank. Chargebacks result in immediate account suspension while the dispute is investigated, which is disruptive for everyone. We resolve nearly all billing disputes directly within 2 business days.
