# Pricing and Plans

CloudNest offers four plans:

## Free
- 5 GB storage
- Up to 3 devices
- Basic file sharing (link-only, no permissions)
- Community support
- Cost: $0 forever

## Personal — $4.99/month
- 200 GB storage
- Unlimited devices
- Advanced sharing with permissions and expiry dates
- Email support (48-hour response)
- 30-day file version history

## Pro — $9.99/month
- 2 TB storage
- Everything in Personal
- Priority email and chat support (24-hour response)
- 180-day file version history
- Advanced search with full-text indexing
- Offline access on mobile

## Business — starts at $15/user/month, minimum 3 users
- Unlimited storage
- Centralized admin console
- SAML SSO and SCIM provisioning
- Audit logs and compliance reporting
- Dedicated account manager (10+ users)
- 1-year file version history
- 24/7 phone support

## Billing

Billing is monthly or annual. Annual billing gives a 20% discount. We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Business plans can also pay by invoice with NET-30 terms.

You can upgrade at any time — the change is prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Refunds are issued within 14 days of payment for any reason, no questions asked.
