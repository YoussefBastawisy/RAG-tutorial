# Account Management and Password Reset

## Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your CloudNest password, you can reset it in two ways:

1. From the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link valid for 30 minutes.
2. From inside the app, go to Settings → Security → Change Password. You will need to enter your current password.

If you do not receive the password reset email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. If it is still missing, contact support — your email may be on our delivery suppression list due to a previous bounce.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

CloudNest supports 2FA via authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password) and SMS. We strongly recommend authenticator apps over SMS for security.

To enable 2FA: Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication → Enable. You will be shown a QR code to scan with your authenticator app, and 10 backup codes. Save the backup codes somewhere safe — without them, losing your authenticator device may permanently lock you out of your account.

## Changing Your Email Address

You can change the email associated with your account at Settings → Profile → Email. After entering a new email, both the old and new addresses will receive a confirmation email. The change is only finalized once you click the link in the new address.

## Account Deletion

To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Account deletion is permanent after a 30-day grace period — during this window, you can sign in to cancel the deletion. After 30 days, all your files, sharing links, and account data are permanently erased and cannot be recovered.
